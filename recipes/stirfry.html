Stir-Fry

Stir-fry is a quick and easy dish made with a variety of vegetables and protein, cooked in a savory sauce. It's a versatile meal that's perfect for busy weeknights. This recipe is a family favorite and is sure to become a staple in your home.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast, sliced thin
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, sugar, and ginger. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add garlic, onion, bell pepper, broccoli, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return chicken to the skillet. Pour in the sauce mixture and stir to coat. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Serve stir-fry over cooked rice.
